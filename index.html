ABOUT

Abdallah Suddun Construction Company Ltd (ASCCL) was founded by Abdallah Suddun circa 2018 and holds a diploma in Principles of Construction and boasts over five years of experience in the field.

The company aims to deliver perfectly crafted luxury homes to the Mauritian public using a pleasant combination of innovative techniques and a sizable hint of affordability hereby creating the ultimate experience for the new homeowner.
